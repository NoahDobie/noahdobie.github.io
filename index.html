PWC Readout Display

An Arduino-based display for a Jetski, featuring speed, rpm, and fuel level readouts.

During the early days of the pandemic, I had a lot of free time and decided to tackle a project I had been thinking about for a while - upgrading the gauges on my Seadoo (PWC). The old, original gauges were non-functional, so I wanted to create a digital display that would gather information from the engine and use a GPS speedometer.

I began designing the circuitry and planning everything around an Arduino, as well as a 3D printed case that would fit perfectly where the old gauges were.

Although money was tight, I reached out to a mentor from my high school who helped me navigate the circuit design and engineering aspects. We worked together to create a design for a GPS speedometer module, a fuel level sensor, and an RPM gauge.

Although I wasn't able to complete the build, this project sparked my passion for the intersection of technology and engineering. It was a valuable learning experience and I hope to continue working on similar projects in the future.
